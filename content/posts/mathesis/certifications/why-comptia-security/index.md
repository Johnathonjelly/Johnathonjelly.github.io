## Why Security?

I'm not sure what possessed me to get the CompTia Security+ certification. One day I woke up completely obsessed with network security. I began reading articles, signing up for security news letters, watching documentaries, and more. Soon I began thinking I could completely career switch into the field of security. It's not so simple though. The field of security is vast and specialized. I knew I needed to set myself apart from other canidates.
